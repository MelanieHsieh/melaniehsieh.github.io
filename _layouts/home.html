In Short

The purpose of this monthly blog is to document my journey on building all sorts of useless but fun projects from scratch. Through my process of learning, coding, test-running and failing, I hope to introduce others to different fields and aspects of STEM.


Why

I started this blog in 2020 when I was 16. Since 2018, I started to program small projects like simple websites for my friends, a vocabulay slideshow powered by javascript and many more. I began to develop a huge interest in technology and engineering. I hope to use this platform to introduce more girls to tech and engineering.
